# Codex review needs human judgment

The learner explained Codex change review as a chain: the summary is only Codex's description, the diff is the factual change, tests and manual checks are evidence, and the final accept/fix/revert decision remains human judgment. They also identified a useful boundary when asking Codex to help review: ask it to inspect, explain, and suggest, not to continue editing.

**Evidence**: The learner described checking summary, diff scope, deletions, unrelated refactors, global style changes, dependency upgrades, removed tests, and verification before deciding whether to accept, partially accept, fix, or revert.
